Halloumi Tacos

Description

A brilliantly zesty vegetarian alternative to tacos. Very quick and easy to make!

This is the perfect mid-week meal when you're really pressed for time but still wanting a tasy, cheap, and healthy meal.

Ingredients

  • 1-2 packs of halloumi
  • 8 tortillas
  • 1 can of black beans
  • 1 tbsp of tomato puree
  • 1-2 clove of garlic
  • 1-2 tbsp of chipotle paste
  • 200ml water
  • 1 lime
  • 1 tbsp of oil
  • Mayo
  • Cilantro (garnish)
  • Hot sauce (optional)

Steps

  1. Cut the halloumi into slices or chunks, depending on your preference
  2. Crush or mince the garlic
  3. Zest the lime
  4. Drain the black beans. Divide it into two portions, and mash one portion with a fork
  5. Heat the oil over medium heat, and add the garlic + tomato puree. Cook for 1 minute until fragrant
  6. Add the water, both portions of black beans and the chipotle paste. Simmer for about 10 minutes
  7. Cook the halloumi in a bit of oil
  8. While the bean mixture and halloumi cook, you can use this time to make the lime + chipotle mayo. Add the lime zest + some chipotle paste to mayo, to taste
  9. You can heat the tortillas if desired
  10. Finally, assemble the tacos as desired. For best results, add mayo, then halloumi, topped by beans and any other toppings like the cilantro or hot sauce
  11. Enjoy!
