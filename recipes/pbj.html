Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich

Description

The classic lunch time classic from your childhood. No mess, no fuss!

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • Peanut butter
  • Jelly

Steps

  1. Spread peanut butter on one side of the bread
  2. Spread jam on the other slice of bread
  3. Put the sandwich together and eat it
